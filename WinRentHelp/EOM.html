End of Month Processing

Winrent maintains Arrears and Transaction records for all contracts in the system. These records are simply historical records and are not needed to maintain balances etc in the system. Althogh the routine is called "Monthly" there is no actual requirement to perform this processs

The "Monthly" routine will allow you to remove old Arrears and Transaction records from the contracts. You could choose (for example) to remove all records older that 3 years. This would be done on a "ad-hoc" basis.

Note that once the records are processed they cannot be retreived except by restoration from a backup file. Therefore, we recommend that you perform a backup before executing this routine.

Winrent will prompt with last dates for both record types. Enter the dates and Winrent will ask request confirmation before proceeding.
